EcoTrace

Our mission for lower emissions.

EcoTrace enables the user to view the environmental impact the products they buy have, and offer more eco-friendly alternatives. The user simply has to scan a barcode with their webcam or phone camera, and information about the origin of the product, alongside a map of the journey that the product has been on to arrive at the store, reducing CO2 emissions significantly.
